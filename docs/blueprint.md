# **App Name**: HybridX Hub

## Core Features:

- Training Plan Showcase: Display training plans for running, cardio, and weight training, focusing on Hybrid Training methodologies.
- Amazon Book Promotion: Prominently feature paper training plan books available on Amazon.
- App Promotion: Directly promote the existing training plan app (app.hybridx.club) with a clear call to action.
- Contact Form: Include a contact form for users to submit queries and feedback.
- FAQ Section: Implement an FAQ section addressing common questions about Hybrid Training and available products.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Black (#000000) for a strong, modern feel.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) for a clean and spacious design.
- Accent color: Yellow (#FFDA63) for calls to action and highlighting important information, contrasting effectively with the black and white.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, techy feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) paired with Space Grotesk, ensuring readability and complementing the headlines.
- Use simple, geometric icons related to running, cardio, and weight training, in black or yellow.
- A clean, modular layout with clear sections for training plans, books, and app promotion.
- Subtle animations (e.g., smooth scrolling, button hovers) to enhance user experience without being distracting.
